Who Are We

STEAME Logic is an educational consulting firm that specializes in assisting
school districts, colleges, government and corporate organizations in the development
and revitalization of their K-12 outreach in Science, Technology, Engineering, Art,
Mathematics and Exercise. In addition, with provide exceptional learning experiences for
K-12 students.

What We Do

We provide personalized STEAME focused programs and activities for your organization.
Our professional have served various government, collegiate and K-12 entities. Some of
our work include:
K-12 STEAME Program Development
Curriculum Development
Speaking Engagements
Teacher Professional Development
Summer Camps
After School Programs


What We Exercise

Child hood obesity is a serious problem in the United States. According to the CDC, there are over 13.7 million children and adolescents effected by obesity. Here at STEAME Logic,
we believe that a healthy body, constitutes a healthy mind. According to the CDC, in America, Obesity is 3.9% among 2- to 5-year-olds, 18.4% among 6- to 11-year-olds, and 20.6% among
12- to 19-year-olds. In South Carolina, in youth under the age of 18, 15% are overweight and 16.7% are obese. In order to decrease these numbers, we are developing a program that will
combine the STEAM learning aspect to Exercise, which will provide our youth an overall healthy development.
